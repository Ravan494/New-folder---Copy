LEADING THE WAY

End to End Handling with at
High-Tech Processing Centers

At Raj Spices, we take pride in our comprehensive approach to spice processing, ensuring quality and authenticity at every step. Our state-of-the-art processing centers are equipped with the latest technology to handle spices from the moment they are harvested to the final packaging. This end-to-end handling allows us to maintain strict quality control, preserve the natural flavors and aromas of our spices, and deliver premium products to our customers worldwide.

Explore our comprehensive processing cycle below to see how we maintain these standards at every stage.

01

Raw Material Procurement

Direct crop procurement from selective region for each product.

02

Storage

Our state-of-the-art processing and storage unit is designed to efficiently handle and store various products while preserving their aroma and color

03

Cleaning & Sorting

Color sorter machines ensure a uniform grade of raw material, which is then handpicked for 99.95% purity. We use advanced machines to maintain color and aroma of the products during the cleaning process.

04

Testing & Analysis

A common practice for finished goods, where every batch is tested to ensure 100% natural products

05

Packaging

Our fully automated mechanised bulk & retail packaging offers tailored packaging & private labeling for various clients.

06

Quality Inspection

Team members conduct rigorous quality checks at three levels, ensuring comprehensive oversight of product quality.

07

Transportation & Delivery

Our trusted network of transporters ensures timely delivery of goods at your doorstep without any damage.
