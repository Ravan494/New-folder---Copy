About Us

Bringing spice to your life

For over two decades, Raj Spices has been at the heart of India’s spice industry specialising in sourcing, processing, and distributing best quality spices to customers across the world. Built on the core pillars of Purity, Authenticity, and Fair Trade, our philosophy reflects the true spirit of India’s spice history.

Our integrated operations adhere to FSSAI norms, ensuring consistency and reliability across the entire supply chain from farm to packaging. By creating an efficient bridge between small farmers and global consumers, we help improve farmers’ livelihoods while supplying the world with highest quality of natural produce.

As an independent and agile organisation, Raj Spices stays responsive to market dynamics and supply chain challenges, without ever compromising on quality or values. Together, with our business partners and farming communities, we aim to build a sustainable future for our people, our customers, and our planet.

Coriander Seeds

Coriander, Coriandrum sativum is an annual herb in the family Apiaceae. Both the leaves and fruit (seeds) of coriander are used as food and medicine. Known for their citrusy, slightly sweet flavor, these seeds are essential in many spice blends.

Cumin Seeds

Cumin, Cuminum cyminum, is a flowering plant in the family Apiaceae native to the Middle East and India. These aromatic seeds have an earthy, nutty flavor and are a cornerstone of Indian cuisine. They're known for their digestive properties and rich antioxidant content.

Dill Seeds

Dill, Anethum graveolens, is an annual herb belonging to the family Apiaceae, the same family as cumin, coriander, and fennel. Dill seeds are small, oval, and flat with light brown colour and darker ridges running lengthwise. They offer a sweet, slightly bitter taste with hints of anise.

Fennel Seeds

Fennel, Foeniculum vulgare, is a perennial herb in the family Apiaceae. Both the bulb and seeds of fennel are used in culinary and medicinal applications. These licorice-flavored seeds are traditionally used for digestive health and add a sweet, aromatic touch to dishes.

Fenugreek Seeds

Fenugreek, Trigonella foenum-graecum, is an annual herb in the family Fabaceae. Fenugreek seeds are small, hard angular, measuring about 2–5 mm in length. With a bitter yet sweet flavor, these seeds are prized for their medicinal properties and are a staple in traditional medicine.

Our Heritage

The Timeless Legacy of Indian Spices

India’s spice trade has a history that dates back thousands of years. India has been at the heart of the global spice trade for over 5,000 years, earning it the title “Land of Spices”. From ancient times, our spices have been treasured not only for their flavours but also for their medicinal and preservative properties. Civilisations from around the world, including the Greeks, Romans, Arabs, and Chinese, traveled vast distances to acquire the aromatic spices of India.

We believe that our spices are more than just ingredients - they are a part of our culture, our heritage, and our way of life. They carry the flavours of home, the essence of tradition, and the promise of health and well-being.

As we move forward, we remain deeply connected to our roots while embracing modern advancements to meet the needs of today’s health conscious consumers. With Raj Spices, you don’t just buy a product you experience the legacy of generations, the promise of authenticity, and the timeless taste of India’s spice heritage.
